# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of the RingNet CPBX Modmail Bot pledge to make participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

---

## Our Standards

The following behaviors contribute to a positive environment:

- Using respectful and professional language
- Being open to constructive feedback and differing viewpoints
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the community it serves
- Showing courtesy and respect toward other contributors

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments of any kind
- Publishing private or sensitive information without consent
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or contributions
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair action in response to any behavior that does not meet these standards.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, or other interactions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including the repository, issue tracker, and any communication channels associated with this project.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers directly. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers who do not uphold this Code of Conduct may face consequences as determined by other members of the project leadership.
